What we do

We're committed to making a difference from day one. By integrating an accessible, transparent Eco-Score that empowers consumers with the data they want to make climate friendly decisions, ecoloop gives online sellers the tool they need to differentiate themselves in a competitive market.

Eco-Score

We've developed a transparent Eco-Score that uses the Product Environmental Footprint to meet digital product passport regulatory requirements and awards a customer-friendly Eco-Score.

Reverse Logistics Networked Value-Chain

We don't stop at a rating - we provide a network of online sellers to empower each other through reverse logistics value chain optimization.

AI-Leveraged Efficiency

We leverage AI for optimal efficiency where it's strongest: data processing. We connect human-solution insights to AI for implementation.

An Integrated Solution

We connect these tools with high-touch customer service to provide an unparalleled experience that optimizes for future regulatory requirements and differentiates sellers.

We're Not Waiting

Join us as we visibly shape the European consumer experience and tangibly build sustainability into every decision in a way that demonstrably benefits online sellers.
